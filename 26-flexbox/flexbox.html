Flexbox

The order of flex values

flex: 1 2 auto;
  • 2 is flex-grow.
  • 1 is flex-shrink.
  • auto is flex-basis.

flex-flow and flex-wrap

Before we can use align-content, we have to use:
  • flex-wrap: wrap;
  • flex-flow: rwo or column wrap

Align-content and align-items

Align-content is used to specify the space between or around contents.

align-items is used to set the align-self of the children on the cross Axis.

Align-items or align-self

Justify-content overrides the container of the align-items or align-self.

Flex-basis

There is another commonly-used box model which is to flex-basis, it is box-sizing.
